About CricStatz

The only scoring app your cricket team needs.

CricStatz is a Flutter-powered mobile app that lets anyone become the scorer. Create custom match formats, manage full squads, record every ball, and share a beautiful live scorecard with your teammates — even when you're offline.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Create Matches

Set up custom formats — T20, ODI, Test, or your own rules. Define overs, powerplays, and more.

Squad Management

Add players, assign roles, manage the toss, and handle two-innings logic effortlessly.

Live Scoring

Ball-by-ball updates in real time. Share a live scorecard link with anyone watching remotely.

Stats & Highlights

Full scorecards, batting & bowling figures, partnerships, and match highlights — all auto-generated.

Team Chats

Coordinate with your team in real time. Discuss strategy, share updates, and stay connected during matches.
